For fast, safe, and effective diagnosis of gastrointestinal diseases which cannot be easily achieved by other imaging modalities, SCH is now offering Its services to cover routine diagnostic and therapeutic procedures of the upper and lower gastrointestinal tract. Emergency Services

SCH Emergency Department is composed of a group of expert emergency physicians trained in Emergency Medicine together with Nursing staff adept in the field of Advance Life Support and Triaging . It can handle medical, surgical and emergencies in adults and children 24 hours a day. The latest in diagnostic modalities and therapeutics can be made available to patients on a timely basis.

It has excellent facilities which include intensive care equipment to completely monitor each patient. SCH ICU competent and well verse staff for critical care provides routine care to its patient to ascertains their status of wellness and freedom from illness.
